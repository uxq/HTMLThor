What is HTML Thor?

HTML Thor is a HTML 5 validator built to highlight syntax, semantic, best practice and deprecated errors within code.

Upload your HTML files, directly input your code, or validate an online website.

Our aim is to help newcomers to HTML 5 learn best practice techniques and to ensure that their code is free of validation errors.

HTML Thor was originally developed in 2013 by a team of UQ students. In 2015 Simon and Jonathan from the original development team set out to re-develop the site to increase consistency and to take advantage of their increased web development knowledge.
